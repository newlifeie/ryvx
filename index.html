Our Story

BanyanTree Foods is an employee cooperative born out of our collective love for 'true to soul' Asian food. With an Asian farm producer network carefully built over 25 years, it specialises in bringing forward 100% natural, farm sourced, sustainably produced, specially graded & manufactured Asian food ingredients. It also showcases a carefully curated product range coming from deeply passionate small producer brands. It's direct distribution team presently works with award winning chefs and catering teams worldwide. It works closely with multiple award winning chefs and restaurant teams worldwide to provide a truly exclusive catalogue of food ingredients that will stand out from the run of the mill. Passionate home cooks can now buy BanyanTree Foods product range, as well as products produced by our network of small / artisan / independent producer partner brands, both online and through our fine food shop partners.

Our Quality Promise

Our philosophy at heart is to supply plantation fresh, selective grade, and truly authentic regional Asian food ingredients without cutting corners. As a dedicated Asian foods platform, we strive to bring forward 'true to soul' passion, flavours and freshness out of each and every product in the platform. Today we have 135+ long term trade and catering relationships including multiple Michelin rated and award winning catering teams and trade partners. Tellicherry Black Peppercorns are grown along the Malabar Coast in the southwestern part of India. These peppercorns are vine-ripened and allowed to mature over time to develop a deep and rich flavor. Just before they turn red, the peppercorns are picked from the vine, and the berries tend to turn black as they dry. Commonly used to season meats, poultry, seafood, sauces, vegetables, starches, fruits, and desserts, Tellicherry Black peppercorns are considered to be one of the finest varieties of peppers in the world.
